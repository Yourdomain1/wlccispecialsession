About Me

Welcome to my academic profile! I am an Associate Professor in Computer Science with over a decade of experience in artificial intelligence research. My work focuses on developing innovative AI solutions for healthcare, remote sensing, and privacy-preserving systems.

I have published extensively in top-tier international journals and conferences, supervised numerous PhD and Master's students, and led several international research projects. My research combines theoretical foundations with practical applications, particularly in medical imaging and deep learning.
